05 — Hero Animation Concept

Opening Film Sequence

The hero section is not a banner — it is a cinematic opening sequence. The user arrives and is immediately immersed in the studio experience.

The Arrangement Reveal

A slow-motion film sequence opens with macro detail: a single ranunculus petal, dew catching light. The camera pulls back to reveal hands at work, placing stems with intention into a sculptural arrangement. The lighting shifts from golden morning warmth to the soft glow of the finished piece. Throughout, the brand statement fades in letter by letter: "Refined Floral Design for Intimate Gatherings."

On scroll, the video frame breaks apart into parallax layers — the arrangement continues into the page as the next section is revealed beneath, creating a seamless cinematic transition from video to content.

Layer 1 — Background

Slow-motion video loop (15–20 seconds) of floral arrangement in progress. Soft depth-of-field, warm tones, intimate framing. Compressed to WebM/MP4 with poster image fallback.

Layer 2 — Text Overlay

Cormorant Garamond Light at display scale. Fades in with 1.5s ease after 0.5s delay. Subtle text-shadow for legibility against video. "Refined Floral Design for Intimate Gatherings."

Layer 3 — Navigation

Transparent header with white wordmark and minimal links. Transitions to linen background with slate text on scroll. "Book Your Experience" CTA always visible.

Layer 4 — Scroll Prompt

Gentle animated chevron at bottom center. Pulses with 2s ease-in-out infinite animation. Fades out on first scroll. Inspired by Studio Mondine's anticipation-building scroll cue.

07 — Technical Approach

Built for Performance & Impact

The site will be built as a single-page cinematic experience with scroll-driven animations, optimized media delivery, and seamless integration with booking and analytics platforms.

Animation Engine

GSAP 3.12+ with ScrollTrigger for all scroll-driven cinematic effects. 30 pre-built cinematic modules. Intersection Observer for performance-aware loading. Smooth scroll with momentum.

Media Pipeline

AI-generated hero image via Gemini, animated to video via WaveSpeed Kling v3. Frame extraction with ffmpeg for scroll-locked playback. WebP/AVIF with CDN delivery. Lazy-loaded galleries.

Performance Targets

PageSpeed 90+ on mobile. LCP under 2.5 seconds. CLS under 0.1. Video hero with poster fallback for slow connections. Responsive from 375px to 2560px. WCAG 2.1 AA compliance.

Integrations

Stripe or Acuity for workshop booking. Google Analytics 4 with conversion tracking. Meta Pixel for retargeting. Email capture via Mailchimp/ConvertKit. Instagram feed API. Google Maps embed.

SEO Strategy

Schema markup for LocalBusiness and FloristService. Location-targeted content: "Floral Workshops Wyckoff NJ," "Florist Bergen County." Google Business Profile optimization. XML sitemap.

Deployment

Vercel edge deployment with global CDN. Custom domain on dreamersjoystudio.com with SSL. Automatic deployments on update. Preview URLs for client review before publish.
