Curated by Palette

A Color-Led System

Rather than choosing individual stems, you choose a palette and scale. From there, every arrangement is composed with seasonal blooms selected for tone, movement, and texture. Scale determines presence — not quality. Every piece is designed with the same refined approach.

Palette tones and floral selections shift seasonally based on availability. Vessels are thoughtfully selected and remain consistent in scale and overall aesthetic, though specific styles may vary.
