The Studio

Where Intention Meets Artistry

DreamersJoy Floral Studio is a private floral design studio built around intention, creative expression, and intimate scale. We craft seasonal arrangements, curated installations, and bespoke floral pieces for discerning clients across Bergen County.

Founded by Sarah De Jesus, our studio operates by appointment only. No public storefront, no walk-ins. This intentional privacy allows us to focus entirely on the craft, sourcing premium seasonal stems and designing with editorial precision.

We also host invitation-based floral experiences for small groups, but our foundation is the art of the arrangement itself. Every stem selected with care. Every piece designed to elevate your space.

Quiet confidence. Contained joy.

The Founder

Sarah De Jesus

Founder & Creative Director

What began as DreamersJoy Events & Design evolved into something more focused, more intentional, and more aligned with Sarah's creative vision. DreamersJoy Floral Studio was born from a desire to create design-forward arrangements and intimate floral experiences.

Sarah's approach to floral design is editorial and sculptural. She favors intentional negative space, textural movement, and understated luxury. Every arrangement reflects her belief that beauty lies in restraint.

Operating by appointment only in Bergen County, she serves discerning clients across Franklin Lakes, Ridgewood, and the surrounding communities who value privacy and exceptional craft.
