The Studio Series

By Invitation Only

The Studio Series is a collection of curated floral evenings held in intimate, intentionally designed settings.

Each experience is centered around a seasonal palette, guiding the materials, mood, and overall atmosphere. Guests are welcomed into a refined environment to design, connect, and create, balancing composition, color, and movement.

These are immersive, design-led gatherings created to feel personal, elevated, and memorable.

Up to 14 guests · Held once or twice monthly · Invitation-based
