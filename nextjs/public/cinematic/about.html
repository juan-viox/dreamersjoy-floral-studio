The Studio & Sarah

A private floral design studio defined by controlled color, editorial restraint, and intentional composition.

The Studio

Where Intention Meets Design

DreamersJoy Floral Studio is a refined, color-driven floral studio based in Bergen County, New Jersey; rooted in intention, design, and intimate scale.

Each collection is developed through a curated palette, inspired by seasonal tones and evolving color studies. Our work balances softness and structure, creating arrangements that feel elevated, modern, and quietly expressive.

We offer seasonal floral collections, hand-tied bouquets, private gatherings, subscriptions, and our signature Studio Series; invitation-only experiences designed to feel immersive, thoughtful, and visually distinct.

Founded by Sarah De Jesus, the studio operates by appointment only. With no public storefront, this intentional privacy allows for a more focused and considered approach to the work.

Each piece is created in limited quantities using seasonally selected blooms, chosen for their movement, texture, and tone; preserving the integrity of every design.

Quiet confidence. Contained joy.

The Founder

Sarah De Jesus

Founder & Creative Director

What began as DreamersJoy Events & Design evolved into something more focused, more intentional, and more aligned with Sarah's creative vision. DreamersJoy Floral Studio was born from a desire to create design-forward arrangements and intimate floral experiences.

Sarah's approach to floral design is artful and sculptural. She favors intentional negative space, textural movement, and understated luxury. Every arrangement reflects her belief that beauty lies in restraint.

Operating by appointment only in Bergen County, she serves discerning clients across Franklin Lakes, Ridgewood, and the surrounding communities who value privacy and exceptional craft.
